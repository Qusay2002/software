Reports

Cost Analysis

This report provides a detailed breakdown of energy costs across the campus. It includes:

  • Total energy expenditure by type (electricity, water, gas).
  • Cost comparisons between different buildings and departments.
  • Trend analysis to identify periods of high expenditure.

Graphs and charts visualize cost data for better understanding and decision-making.

Sustainability Metrics

This report tracks the campus's sustainability performance. It includes:

  • Carbon footprint calculations based on energy consumption.
  • Percentage of energy derived from renewable sources.
  • Progress towards sustainability goals and benchmarks.

Detailed metrics are presented to show how the campus is contributing to environmental sustainability.

Designing Complete and Packaged Reports

This section describes how to create comprehensive and packaged reports for various stakeholders. It includes:

  • Customizable templates for different types of reports.
  • Tools for aggregating data from multiple sources.
  • Options for exporting reports in various formats (PDF, Excel, etc.).

Designed reports can be shared with decision-makers, management, and other relevant parties to support informed actions and strategies.
